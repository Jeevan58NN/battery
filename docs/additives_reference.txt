LIST OF ELECTROLYTE ADDITIVES AND THEIR EFFECTS

FEC (Fluoroethylene Carbonate)
- Improves SEI for silicon
- Reduces lithium plating

VC (Vinylene Carbonate)
- SEI stabilizer for graphite
- Improves cycle life

LiDFOB
- Enhances high-voltage stability
- Used for >4.3V cells

PES Additive
- Must for 4.7V high-voltage LMO cells

TMSP Additive
- Protects cathode at high temperature
