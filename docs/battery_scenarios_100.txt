Scenario 1:
Anode = Graphite  
Electrolyte = EC/DMC + LiPF6  
Temp = 25°C  
Required = 5V, 3A  

Answer:
Use LNMO cathode (4.7–5.0V).  
Add LiBOB + LiFSI additives.  
Reduce cathode thickness to 55 µm.  
Use ceramic separator.
